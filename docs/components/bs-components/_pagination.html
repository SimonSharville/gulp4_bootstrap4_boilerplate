SARD Variations

Pagination sard

Add .pagination-sard to code .pagination and it will add Arrow Icons to the Previous and Next page-links,
This relies on the aria-label being either Previous or Next.

Pagination Sard will change it's colours depending on the theme.

When including paginination within one of these, add .pagination-div to the nav . You can also add .pagination-entries within that nav.

Within these the pagination will alway range on the right.
