Collapsable Cards/Panels

Collapsable Cards or Panels are single panels that are collapsable. If you have multiples of they will all open or collapse individually, unlike Accordions (see bellow), that are grouped and will close when another is opened.

Add the class of .collapsable to the .card as well as the usual collapse elements inside.
