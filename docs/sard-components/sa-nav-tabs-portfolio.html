Dataset advice

Evidence should be added for each section in your portfolio, then the section marked as ‘Complete’. You cannot submit your appraisal unless every section in your portfolio is marked as ‘In Progress’, ‘Complete’ or ‘Not Applicable’.
Click on the buttons below to change the status.

Evidence

Please enter a description of the evidence you have for this section. You can either upload a document or just make a note of any paper-based evidence that you may have and click ‘Save Evidence’. Setting an expiry date will ensure that any out of date evidence will no longer be included in following appraisals. N.B. Setting an expiry date will not remove the document after this date.
