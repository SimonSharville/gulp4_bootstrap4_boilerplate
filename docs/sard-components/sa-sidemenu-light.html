Sard Side Menu - Light

This is identical to the default sidemenu but with a light colouring

Simply add .sidemenu-light to .sa-sidemenu

On screen sizes larger than 1200px, the Sard sidemenu pushes out from the left.

On screen sizes less than 1200px, the sidemenu slides over the content.

To build this correctly, the .sa-sidemenu.sidemenu-light must be followed directly by either a .container-fluid or a .container . that contains the main contents of the site.

You can have multiple containers one after the other...
...if you wish
