Links with specific icons

Link icons can be place directly onto the link, or on the containing paragraph or span. They should have the class of .link-icon and either .link-NAME or .NAME . i.e .link-icon.link-pdf or .link-icon.pdf . They are designed so that the text of the link hangs from the icon.
