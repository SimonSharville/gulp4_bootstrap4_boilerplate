Links with specific icons

Link icons can be place directly onto the link, or on the containing paragraph or span. They should have the class of .link-icon and either .link-NAME or .NAME . i.e .link-icon.link-pdf or .link-icon.pdf . They are designed so that the text of the link hangs from the icon.

Examples

File Types

You can use any of the file extensions within the brackets ( ).

AUDIO
.link-icon.link-audio or .link-icon.audio

EXCEL (xls, xlsx)
.link-icon.link-xls or .link-icon.xls

IMAGES (gif, ico, image, jpg, photo, png )
.link-icon.link-image or .link-icon.image

PDF
.link-icon.link-pdf or .link-icon.pdf

POWERPOINT (ppt, pptm, pptx)
.link-icon.link-ppt or .link-icon.ppt

TEXT (txt, odt, rtf)
.link-icon.link-txt or .link-icon.txt

VIDEO
.link-icon.link-video or .link-icon.video

WORD (doc, docm, docx)
.link-icon.link-doc or .link-icon.doc

ZIP (zip, archive)
.link-icon.link-zip or .link-icon.zip
