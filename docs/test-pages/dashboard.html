Getting Started

To get started, click on the ‘New Appraisal’ button in the Appraisals section. This will create 2 new pages: My Appraisal and My Portfolio.

01 Update Portfolio Evidence should be added for each section of your portfolio, then the section marked as green, amber or N/A.

Appraisal Preparation Sections 1 to 18 of your appraisal should be filled in. Once you’ve completed a section you need to click on ‘Mark As Complete’.

Send to Appraiser When Sections 1 to 18 have been marked as completed and all sections of the portfolio have been marked green, amber or N/A, you can submit the appraisal to your appraiser.

Agree PDP and statements The appraiser is responsible for Section 19 and Section 20 of your appraisal. These should be completed during or after the appraisal meeting.

Appraiser Sign-Off and Doctor Sign-Off Section 21 should be completed by the appraiser. Section 22 should be completed by the doctor.

Submit to RO Once the appraisal is complete, the appraiser can submit it to the responsible officer. No changes can be made after this point.

Patient Questionnaire

Licensed doctors are expected to seek feedback from colleagues and patients and review and act upon that feedback where appropriate.

The purpose of this exercise is to provide doctors with information about their work through the eyes of those they work with and treat, and is intended to help inform their further development.

Please do not write your name on this questionnaire.

Please base your answers only on the consultation you have had today.

Please mark the box like this [✓] with a ball point pen. If you change your mind just cross out your old response and make your new choice.

  • KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS FOR FILLING IN THIS FORM:
  • Press Alt or the Option key to switch on the keyboard shortcuts which should appear in red.
  • Press Tab to scroll through each question. Hold Shift and Tab to go back a question.
  • Type a number to toggle the checkboxes or radio buttons on and off.
  • Use the arrow keys to scroll through select boxes, and hit enter to choose an option.
  • You can click the mouse at any time.
